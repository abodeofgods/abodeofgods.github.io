Project Overview

Abode of Gods is a sci-fi narrative project set in a divergent timeline where global history reshaped civilization and technology advanced into a fusion of AI dominance and mythological futurism.

The story follows Dev Arya, an astronaut caught between a machine-controlled Earth and a cosmic dimension beyond space-time.
